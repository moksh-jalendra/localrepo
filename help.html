Platform Guide

üöÄ How It Works

1. Play & Record
Open the Casio tab to play freely. Switch to "Track Mode" to record your melody. You can also change instruments (Tone) to Flute or Synth.
2. Weekly Challenges
Visit the Hunt tab to join the Weekly Challenge. Listen to a verified track, then play it back. You get unlimited practice but only one chance to score points!
3. Learn with AI
Chat with Mitra Bot in the AI Tutor tab. Ask for music theory tips or request simple melodies to practice.
4. Share Music
Go to your Profile to see your saved tracks. Click "Share" to post them to the public Feed so others can listen and like.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get verified? ‚ñº
Verification is manually approved. To apply, you must have at least 5 original tracks saved and be active in the weekly challenges. Contact support for review.
How do Challenge points work? ‚ñº
Points are calculated based on your timing accuracy when playing back a Challenge song. The leaderboard resets every week, giving everyone a fresh start!
Can I download my songs? ‚ñº
Currently, songs are saved to your cloud profile. We are working on an MP3 export feature for a future update!
