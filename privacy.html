Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 26, 2025

At PianoMitra, accessible from pianomitra.online, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by PianoMitra and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Account Information: When you register via Firebase Authentication, we store your email address and display name to manage your profile.
  • User Content: Audio tracks, custom tones, and social posts you create are stored in our Firebase Realtime Database.
  • Local Storage: We use your browser's local storage to save your preferences (Dark/Light mode) and cache feed data for better performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our Service (saving your music).
  • To allow you to participate in interactive features (Social Feed, Likes, Leaderboards).
  • To generate AI music suggestions via the Google Gemini API (Mitra Bot).

3. Google Gemini AI

Our "AI Tutor" feature utilizes the Google Gemini API. When you interact with the AI chat, your text queries are sent to Google's servers for processing. We do not send personal identifiable information (PII) to the AI.

4. Cookies

Like any other website, PianoMitra uses "cookies". These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences and authentication state.

5. Data Deletion

You have the right to request the deletion of your data. You can delete individual tracks and posts directly from your Profile page.

6. Contact Us

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at: moksh@pianomitra.online
