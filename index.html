Master the Virtual Piano Online

Welcome to PianoMitra, the premier browser-based music studio designed for musicians of all skill levels. Unlike traditional software that requires heavy downloads, our online virtual piano utilizes advanced Web Audio API technology to deliver low-latency, high-fidelity sound directly in Chrome, Edge, or Safari. Whether you are practicing chords, composing a new melody, or simply having fun, our tool transforms your keyboard into a versatile musical instrument.

üéπ How to Play

PianoMitra maps your computer's QWERTY keyboard to musical notes. The middle row (A-S-D-F...) corresponds to the white keys of the central octave, while the top row (W-E-T-Y...) triggers the black keys (sharps and flats). This intuitive layout allows you to play complex chords and melodies with ease. Toggle the "ABC Keys" button in the studio to see the visual mapping.

ü§ñ AI Music Tutor

Stuck on a melody? Our integrated Mitra Bot is powered by Google's Gemini AI. It acts as your personal music theory assistant. You can ask it to generate simple practice scales, explain the Circle of Fifths, or suggest chord progressions for your next song. It bridges the gap between coding and creativity.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this virtual piano free to use?

Yes, PianoMitra is 100% free. You can access all features, including the synthesizer tones (Sine, Square, Sawtooth) and the recording functionality without any subscription.

Can I save my recordings?

Absolutely. By logging in with your Google account, you can save your tracks to your cloud profile. Your data is securely stored using Firebase Realtime Database, ensuring you never lose a melody.

Does it work on mobile/tablets?

Yes! The application is fully responsive. On mobile devices, simply rotate your screen to landscape mode for the best playing experience. The touch interface supports multi-touch, allowing you to play chords just like on a real piano.
